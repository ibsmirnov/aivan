What is Generative AI

The goal of this section is to explain and demystify all these acronyms that we are now surrounded by: AI, ML, LLM. While it might be tempting to jump straight into using chatbots like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or others, having a conceptual understanding of the underlying technologies will help you become a more effective user and enable you to critically evaluate claims about their capabilities and limitations.
