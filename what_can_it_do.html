What can it do

Is there a place for AI in research? Well, just last year, two Nobel Prizes were awarded for AI applications in researchArtificial Intelligence awarded two Nobel Prizes for innovations that will shape the future of medicine . The 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry was awarded for developing an AI algorithm that solved the 50-year protein structure prediction challenge, and in Physics it was awarded for inventions that enable machine learning with artificial neural networks, which as we discussed is a technology that underpins large language models. So, Generative AI could definitely be a powerful assistant in your research journey. In this section, we explore how GenAI can be helpful at different stages of the research cycle.
